About Me

I am a web developer and maker of cool stuff living and working in Chicago.

When I'm not writing code, solving problems, or experimenting, I like to spend my time enjoying this beautiful city with my amazing wife and our two year old son.

Together, we enjoy travelling and trying new restaurants and breweries (we have visited more than 70 breweries and brewpubs across the United States and abroad!) At home, I love to brew fine ales and lagers of my own design, which are served from a custom draft system I devised and built.
